Personalized or relationship-oriented subjects ('Thank You + Quick Feedback Request', 'Welcome + Member Kit') achieve the highest open rates (58-76%), though these were sent to very small lists (17-29 recipients) which inflates rates. Many high-open-rate emails had 0% click rates (e.g., 'New Analytic reports' series, 'Now live: A faster, cleaner provider profile experience'), indicating informational announcements are opened but not clicked. Data is insufficient to evaluate specific CTA button text or placement.
